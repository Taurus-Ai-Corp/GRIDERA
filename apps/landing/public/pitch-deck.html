07. INVESTOR Q&A — TECHNICAL

Technical Objections

Part 1 of 3

Q: Can Ethereum or Solana just fork to PQC?

A: Not quickly. Larger signatures increase storage and bandwidth. That is why GRIDERA does not try to rebuild consensus; we filter, wrap, and migrate payloads upstream so downstream systems receive clean, regulator-checkable inputs.

Q: Is quantum still far away?

A: "Harvest now, decrypt later" is happening today. Compliance deadlines are not waiting for a quantum computer. CNSA 2.0 and EU guidance already set transition windows.

Q: How do you keep throughput usable?

A: We sign evidence, not every micro-transaction. HCS gives us sub-second finality on proof anchors without bloating the chain with large payloads.
